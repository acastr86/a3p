Integration Services

When a service which you need to run your business cannot be directly integrated using the automation facilities that the platform has, we are readiness to offer you the integration service customized for each case.

Our group of experts will first try to create semantics in the system you need to access, if that is the case your development time and costs will drop considerably; a functionality test will tell if converting the integrate semantic interfaces the service is totally reusable.

The integration process carries a set of standardized steps with the aim of any future changes in the most cases can be done on your side without any intervention from our team.

Semantically

Initially a group of analysts oriented to your market segment will meet with you to better understand your Applications and create semantics definitions.

Development (if necesary)

Our development group will provide the necessary integrations for integration with your Applications you need to keep using.

Testing

Finally you and your staff can test each of the Applications until you feel confident that they are full integrated.

Training

The training stage allows you to better understand everything that was implemented and to be able to make changes without need external involvement.

Continuos Support

When you use the integration services your system is automatically secured for future supports for any inconvenience that may arise, as long as these are not associated to external tools in which case a review must be requested to reach a new integration contract.

The integration is guaranteed even to any future update of our systems if that were the case, we promise keep your systems working if you have upgraded to a new version with some changes.
