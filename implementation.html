Implementation Services

When you need that, you need help to make the transformation of your company happen, but do you don't have the staff with the necessary experience or you need to start the transformation process as soon as possible, we have the ideal solution for you.

Our implementation service has experts in each branch that will be able to put into operation with the transformation of your company with the use of the appropriate technology in record time; our team follows a methodological process of phases to ensure that you can translate your intentions and problems with the objective that this service is easy to modify by your own staff once it is configured.

Planing

Initially a group of analysts oriented to your market segment will meet with you to better understand your systems.

Modeling

Our modeling experts will shape all your systems.

Development

Our development group will provide the necessary integrations for integration with your Applications you need to keep using.

Testing

Finally you and your staff can test each of the systems until you feel confident in its use.

Training

The training stage allows you to better understand everything that was implemented and to be able to make changes without need external involvement.

Continuos Support

When you use the implementation services your system is automatically secured to future supports for any inconvenience that may arise, as long as these are not associated to external tools in which case a review must be requested to reach a new implementation contract.

The implementation is guaranteed even to any future update of our systems if that were the case, we promise keep your systems working if you have upgraded to a new version with some changes.
