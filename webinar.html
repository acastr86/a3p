Foundations of a3p Modeling

Every company needs to use many class of system for the proper function of the business whether these manuals, computerized or a hybrid between the two; so far there is no easy way to be able to shape the policies, processes and procedures in which these systems should be based so that they are easily transferable and understandable between workers, and at the same time are interpreted by the computer to understand the semantics and intention of them.

In this webinar, join a3p reserach to learn the fundamentals of a3p Modeling. You will learn how to translate your systems in a declarative and being able to define the goals and objectives that are pursued in each policy created.

In the registration form it is important that you specify the business sector in which your company performs, that way we can guide the examples that we will show in the webinar to the sectors that have the most public participating in the webinar.

Equally important is that you specify any prior questions you may have during the webinar for that way address thems within the presentation, however there will be a question section, but only those that time allows, hence the importance of preparing them at the time of registration.
